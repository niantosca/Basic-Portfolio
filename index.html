Born and raised in Richmond, VA, I attended Trinity Episcopal High School and am a graduate of Virginia Commonwealth University. I earned a degree in Bachelor of Arts in Foreign Language with a concentration in Spanish. Dissatisfied with the opportunities afforded by this degree, I quickly realized that I needed to further my education. After discovering programming and fostering a growing interest in the field during my personal time, I felt I needed more structured instruction. I found and enrolled in the University of Richmond Coding Bootcamp, making one of the best decisions of my life. Through the program, I became well versed in all the necessary technologies in order to become a Full-Stack Web Developer. As a graduate of the program I received a Certificate in Web Development, and am searching for an entry into the industry that will launch me on what I expect to be a life-long career in the field.
